# 🍌 Statistical Analysis and Forecasting of Banana Production: Global to Local Study

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive statistical analysis of banana production from a global, national (India), state, and district perspective. It explores historical trends, production patterns, productivity, cultivation area, and price movements while forecasting future banana production using Time Series Analysis (ARIMA models).

The study combines agricultural statistics with data analytics to provide meaningful insights for researchers, policymakers, and agricultural planners.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze historical trends in global and Indian banana production.
* Compare banana production across countries, Indian states, and Maharashtra districts.
* Study changes in cultivation area, production, and productivity.
* Forecast future banana production using ARIMA time series models.
* Analyze monthly banana price trends across major producing states.
* Review recent research related to banana production and forecasting.

---

## 📊 Dataset

The project uses secondary data collected from:

* Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO)
* National Horticulture Board (NHB)
* Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India
* APEDA and other publicly available agricultural datasets

---

## 🛠️ Methodology

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Trend Analysis
* CAGR Calculation
* Pareto Analysis
* Time Series Analysis
* ARIMA Model Selection and Forecasting
* Forecast Validation using Accuracy Measures (RMSE, MAE, MAPE, AIC, BIC)

---

## 📈 Key Analysis

* Global banana production trends (1960–2025)
* Top banana-producing countries
* Banana production, area, and productivity in India
* State-wise production analysis
* District-wise analysis for Maharashtra (Jalgaon, Solapur, and Nanded)
* Monthly banana price analysis
* Five-year production forecasts using ARIMA models

---

## 📌 Key Findings

* India is the world's largest banana producer, contributing approximately **26–27%** of global production.
* Global banana production is projected to increase from around **143 MMT in 2025** to nearly **155 MMT by 2029**.
* Maharashtra remains India's leading banana-producing state, with **Jalgaon** being the "Banana Capital of India."
* India's banana production has shown a consistent upward trend with a CAGR of approximately **2.5%** over the study period.
* ARIMA models effectively captured production trends and produced reliable forecasts for global, national, and regional datasets.
